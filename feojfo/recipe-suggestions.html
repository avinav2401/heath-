Protein-Packed Smoothie

Ingredients: 1 banana, 1 scoop whey protein, 1 cup almond milk, 1 tbsp peanut butter, ice cubes

Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth. Enjoy chilled.

Calories: 280 | Protein: 25g | Carbs: 20g | Fat: 10g

Colorful Quinoa Salad

Ingredients: Cooked quinoa, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red bell pepper, lemon juice, olive oil

Instructions: Toss all veggies with quinoa, drizzle lemon & olive oil. Chill before serving.

Calories: 350 | Protein: 12g | Carbs: 40g | Fat: 14g

Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken breast, garlic, lemon juice, oregano, olive oil

Instructions: Marinate chicken in all ingredients for 1 hour. Grill until cooked.
